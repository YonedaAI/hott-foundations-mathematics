Voevodsky's univalence says: equivalences ARE identities. But equivalences are *symmetric*. What about categories, where morphisms point one way? Directed univalence has been open since 2017. This paper maps the full territory. 🧵 (1/5)

#HoTT #DirectedTypeTheory #CategoryTheory #TypeTheory

---

(2/5) Gratzer-Weinberger-Buchholtz (2024) proved the first complete fragment: Hom_S(A,B) ≃ Fun(A,B) for the *discrete* sub-universe S. This is triangulated type theory (TTT) — a system with flat/sharp/Disc modalities. A landmark result.

---

(3/5) This paper gives a structured account of the full program: Riehl-Shulman 2017 partial result → GWB 2024 discrete case → four candidate complete principles of increasing strength → analysis of the obstructions.

---

(4/5) The obstacles: modal dependence, opposite-type coherence, and the absence of an internal universe of (∞,1)-categories. The complete principle for the *full* universe remains open — but the paper frames it with precision never before achieved.

---

(5/5) Part V of Volume II. Directed univalence is the foundation for synthetic representation theory and the Langlands programme in HoTT — which is where this series is headed.
